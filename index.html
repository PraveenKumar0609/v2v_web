WELCOME TO THE VISION TO VOICE FOR VISUALLY CHALLANGED PEOPLE

VISION TO VOICE transforms exams for the visually challenged by converting questions into audio and transcribing spoken answers into text. We aim to make the exam experience independent and accessible for all.

ABOUT US

Mission

We empower visually challenged individuals with a platform that makes exam-taking accessible and independent. Our technology converts exam content to audio and enables easy recording and submission of answers. We bridge the gap between technology and accessibility, ensuring everyon e can confidently take exams.



Vision

To be the top platform for accessible exams, empowering visually impaired individuals to take exams with confidence and independence. We strive for a seamless and inclusive experience for everyone.

INSTRUCTION

Upload Question Paper

Choose your question paper file in either image or PDF format.then upload it to start the audio output process.

Listen to Questions

The system will read each question aloud, providing clear audio output. Simply listen to the question being read out and prepare your answer.

Speak Your Answer

After listening to the question, speak your answer clearly into the microphone,it will convert your spoken response into text.

Submit for Printing

Once you've answered all questions, review your responses and click “Submit.” Your answers will be prepared for printing.
